Know what’s happening
inside the water

Problems in your ponds or tanks aren’t visible until
 it’s too late. Aquafarm Sensors let you
 know what’s happening beneath the surface by
 monitoring changes every 10 minutes —
 so you can anticipate and resolve problems
 before they happen.

Stay connected to your pond

No matter whether you are marine shrimp
grower or an inland fish farmer you want to know
the conditions in your aquaculture farm.

You’ll never lose
your stocks again

The Aquafarm system alerts you to check about your pond or tank’s water quality when oxygen, temperature or pH levels go out of the pre-set range.

Increase your
farms’ yield

With clear water quality data from your ponds you will be able to make better decisions on feeding schedule and water management that will result in higher success for harvest.

Easy
installation

Upon purchase, we provide you with Aquafarm sensor nodes, which are ready for immediate deployment on site. You just need to insert a local data SIM card into the Aquafarm gateway and make sure that you keep the sensor probes regularly calibrated.

Accurate

Calibration is crucial for providing reliable data to the farmers and accurate alerting. With our easy calibration process you can make sure that your dashboard presents solid data to you.
